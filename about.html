About the Author!

Hello! My name is Robin Luo.

I am a senior studying Computer Science at the University of Rutgers. I transferred last year from the University of Washington, Seattle, where I studied Biology for two years. As a summary, I was born in Connecticut, then moved to New Jersey later on, then moved to Seattle, then back again to New Jersey. I hope to one day study Human Computer Interaction and move again for work in a bigger city.

My interests include Graphic Design, UI/UX, and web development. My current favorite methods of creating are Adobe Illustrator for graphics and MS Paint for drawings. I create designs for Rutgers Calisthenics Club, Rutgers Esports, and Women in Computer Science (WICS). In my free time, I play video games with friends and listen to music. I love tv shows (sitcoms especially), and I have a long list of movies I plan on watching but just have never found the time to.
